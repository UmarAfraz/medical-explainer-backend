RADIOLOGY TERMINOLOGY GLOSSARY
==============================================

Common terms found in imaging reports:

Consolidation: Area where air spaces are filled with fluid/cells
Effusion: Fluid buildup
Pneumothorax: Collapsed lung (air around lung)
Opacity: Area that appears white on X-ray
Lucency: Area that appears dark on X-ray
Cardiomegaly: Enlarged heart
Atelectasis: Partial lung collapse
Nodule: Small round growth
Mass: Larger abnormal growth
Edema: Swelling from fluid
Hemorrhage: Bleeding
Infarct: Dead tissue from lack of blood
Lesion: Abnormal area of tissue
Enhancement: Brightening with contrast dye
Stenosis: Narrowing
Hydronephrosis: Kidney swelling from urine backup
BI-RADS: Breast imaging reporting system (1=normal, 6=proven cancer)
